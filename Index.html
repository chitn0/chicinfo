Info

Process & Payment

  1. Application: After reading the information here, go to the link below & fill out the form.
  2. Confirmation: If your application has been accepted I will confirm the details of your commission via email.
    • At this point you can send additional information or visual references. Eg. Photos, written descriptions, existing art, doodles, stock photos, colour palettes, etc.
    • It is IMPORTANT that the details of the commission are clearly expressed. Being clear from the beginning saves time on revisions in the sketching stage.
  3. Quote: Once I’ve gauged the work that will go into your commission, I will send a quote.
    • Surcharges generally apply if a commission requires more work (eg. extremely detailed armour or clothing). Otherwise prices will follow what I’ve provided below.
    • Upon receiving a quote clients can decide whether they would like to pay for the surcharge or find solutions to suit their budget (ie. adjusting the commission).
  4. Invoice: After the quote has been accepted, I will send an invoice.
  5. Payment: Work will commence once payment has been made either in full or 50% upfront.
    • Currently only using PAYPAL.
    • Full refunds cannot be made past this point.
  6. Sketch: I will send a rough sketch for approval before starting the lineart. Max 2 revisions can be made at this point, but it is important that the initial request is clear from the start.
    • Major changes made after the sketch is approved will incur a fee.
    • Minor changes can be made while lining, but nothing that requires me to redraw significant portions of the work that are already lined.
    • Full refunds cannot be made at this point.
  7. Completed Work: I will typically send the completed work as a high resolution JPEG and PDF unless otherwise asked.
    • If you’ve paid in deposits, I will send a preview first.
    • Finished files will only be sent upon receiving payment in full.

Terms of Service

Refunds & Cancellations

  • Cancellations should ideally be organised prior to payment as refunds will not be given in full once sketches have begun.
  • Disliking the commission or change of mind are not valid reasons for a refund.
  • Full refunds are eligible to clients in the event that I (the artist) cause a delay of more than 4 months since starting work on the commission.
  • Late payments in the form of: 1) a full payment 2) an upfront deposit, or 3) a final deposit may result in the termination of the commission.
  • I reserve the right to reject a commission at any stage of completion of the work. I am not required to provide an explanation for my decision (but will out of courtesy).
  • In the event of cancellation or breach by the Client, the Client shall pay the artist according to the following schedule:
      50% of agreed fee if cancelled before delivery of the initial stage of designs.
      50% of agreed fee if cancelled after delivery of the initial stage of designs.
      50% of agreed fee if cancelled after delivery of the second stage of designs (second stage not always necessary).
      75% of agreed fee if cancelled after delivery of advanced stage of designs.
      100% of agreed fee if cancelled after delivery of finished artwork.

Privacy

Kindly refrain from sharing any personal information (legal name, legal address, etc.) you may find during the process of commissioning me. I would like to remain largely anonymous and under my known alias, Chii. In turn, I will respect your own privacy.

Credit

Sharing of my work online should be given to my Twitter username: @chitn0. Use of my alias (Chii) is optional but should only be done with the addition of my Twitter username.

Commercial Rights

A purchase of commercial rights can be negotiated through email. With commercial rights you are allowed to use your commissioned work for commercial purposes. This includes: making merchandise, using it in books or covers, or promoting your own brand. Credit is still required for usage of the artwork, even if altered or edited.

Delivery

Delivery of a piece may take at least 3 weeks with time divided between other work and commitments. I will do my best to deliver work in a timely manner but will notify you in advance if there is a chance of possible delays. Please let me know in advance if there is a deadline for your work. If the deadline requires a considerable amount of rush on my part, I may charge an overtime fee.
